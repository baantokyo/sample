Sakura-shimmachi town is located 7.2km(4.4mile) south-west of Shibuya.

Sakura-shimmachi is famous for TV animation Sazaesan which has been broadcasted since 1969. Most stories of Sazaesan are based on deep Japanese culture and very private Isono family life in this town.

Sakura-shimmachi Station(DT-5) East-gate

*4th local station from Shibuya on Tokyu Denentoshi-line(DT-line) minits walk.
